Visual Hierarchy

Church News

The Church News website is a good example of the design principle, visual hierarchy, because of the way the designer used a bold background in the header to draw your eyes there first. Then a headings in varying weights to pull your attention to important, or current news first.

Rule of Thirds

Saint Louis Zoo

The Saint Louis Zoo website uses a wide variety of saturated colors and several div elements to create a very visually appealing block layout. This is a good example of the rule of thirds because of the way the designer used these elements to create a unique visual hierarchy and align key features and buttons.

White Space and Clean Design

Nussbaum

It is important to note that white space is not always white. The Nussbaum website is a great example of white space, or empty space, that help bolden and enhance their message. This designer has cleverly placed a video behind the text to create white space that gives you "room to breath" while still providing content.
